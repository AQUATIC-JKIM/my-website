Research Interests

Taxonomy of Myrmeleontidae

I am conducting a taxonomic review of the family Myrmeleontidae Latreille (Neuroptera: Myrmeleontiformia) from the Korean Peninsula. This study highlights the conservation value of this family and provides an updated checklist and morphological descriptions for Korean antlions.

Phylogenomics of Veliidae

My research extends to the phylogenomics of Veliidae (Hemiptera: Gerromorpha). I am planning a comprehensive study using Transcriptome and Anchored Hybrid Enrichment (AHE) methods to resolve phylogenetic relationships within this family, especially focused on the Korean fauna.

Korean Aquatic Insect Fauna

I am also interested in the broader taxonomic review and faunistic study of Korean aquatic insects, including Gerroidea and Corixoidea. My goal is to establish a robust database for the freshwater insect diversity of South Korea.
