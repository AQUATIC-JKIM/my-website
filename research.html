Research Interests

Taxonomy and Evolution of Gerroidea

My primary research focuses on the systematics and evolutionary history of the superfamily Gerroidea.

Korean Insect Fauna and Conservation

My faunistic research specifically targets Aquatic Coleoptera, Aquatic Hemiptera, and the family Myrmeleontidae (Neuroptera). Through taxonomic reviews, I strive to provide crucial data that contributes to the conservation and management of these vulnerable taxa.

Biogeography of East Asia

I investigate the biogeographical patterns of East Asian insects to understand the historical and environmental factors that have shaped the current biota.
